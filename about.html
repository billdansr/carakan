About

A simple website as an introduction to Javanese writing as part of Javanese, and thus Indonesian culture heritage. I thought to myself, why not create a documentation of my own cultural heritage as I am my self a Javanese person that has probably live and spent my whole life in Indonesia and on Java Island to be more specific.

This website is still far from perfect, and by any means it is still incomplete. Therefore, you might learn a bit about Javanese script, but if you are really interested, you might wanted to go through other sources that are much complete and thorough.

This website is initially made as an assignment on Web Development for CS50T to complete the the final challenge, which is to build a website. And voila, I did it by taking an idea to introduce a little bit of Indonesian culture — Javanese writing!

Thanks for visiting!
