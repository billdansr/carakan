Carakan

Carakan (ꦕꦫꦏꦤ꧀) is Javanese script that Javanese people use to write in their language.

In this page, we are going to introduce the Javanese alphabet and show how it is used — how to read it or even write it.

Vowels

Vowels that do not follow consonants has unique letters also known as pure vowels.

Consonants

In writing aksara carakan (Javanese Script), consonants are pronounced with the vowel a by default. For example, the letter ꦏ is always pronunced as ka, not k unless when given a certain diacritic to change the inherent a into another vowel.
